Engineering Excellence Since 2007

Building Industrial Excellence

Eurocore Industrial Company Ltd. is a leading engineering and fabrication company established in 2007, delivering HVAC solutions, structural steel fabrication, industrial maintenance, and global steel trading services across Saudi Arabia. Operating from Dammam and Jubail, we provide reliable industrial solutions tailored to the Kingdom's growing infrastructure and energy sectors.

Driven by quality, safety, and technical excellence, Eurocore serves the oil & gas, petrochemical, mining, power, renewable energy, and construction industries. Through our experienced workforce and strategic global network, we continue to support industrial growth while contributing to the vision of a stronger and more sustainable future.

GLOBAL NETWORK

Global Reach. Local Expertise. INDIA | KSA | UAE

Eurocore Industrial Company Ltd. operates through a highly integrated strategic network spanning India, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates.

This trilateral presence allows us to bridge the gap between global manufacturing powerhouses and the Middle East's most ambitious infrastructure projects.

By maintaining local operational hubs in the KSA and UAE, supported by a robust engineering and legal framework in India, we provide our clients with seamless cross-border logistics, secure trade finance, and localized technical expertise.

Our global reach is the foundation of our ability to deliver large-scale industrial solutions with the speed and reliability that modern energy and construction sectors demand.

Our Values

Eurocore is committed to maintaining a leading position in engineering, steel structure, and mechanical projects by delivering quality services through technical competence, professional expertise, and an unwavering commitment to safety.

We believe customer satisfaction is our greatest asset. By maintaining close relationships with our clients throughout every phase of a project, we ensure their requirements are fully understood and delivered with excellence and reliability.

Our Goals

Our goal is to be recognized as a leading provider of specialized industrial services and products across Saudi Arabia by partnering with world-class organizations and adopting industry best practices.

Through collaboration, technology transfer, and continuous innovation, we strive to create sustainable solutions that contribute to the Kingdom's industrial growth and long-term success.

Quality Statement

Our Commitment to Quality Excellence

EUROCORE recognizes and understands that the full involvement of all employees is necessary to achieve all our goals.
We always strive to provide quality & reliable service to all our clients.

The Management of EUROCORE has the ultimate responsibility for establishing and maintaining quality.

Our quality control measures are implemented in every location through individual Project Managers and Senior Managers.

Quality Control and Quality Assurance Engineers are assigned to each location by Project Managers and Senior Managers.

The Engineers work with Project Managers to ensure standards and specifications are met. Daily briefings reinforce quality objectives.

Internal Non-Conformance Reports provide a common method for identifying quality concerns and implementing corrective actions.

Supervisors initiate corrective actions whenever faults are identified, aiming to eliminate rework and work rejection.
