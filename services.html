Services

Our Services That We Provide

01

HVAC Package & Duct Manufacturing

Eurocore Industrial Company provides HVAC Package & Duct Manufacturing services through state-of-the-art automated production of GI, Stainless Steel, and Aluminum ducting. We specialize in Spiral Round Duct systems and AHU/FCU installations following SMACNA/NFPA standards.



02

Power plant HVAC supply, installation & commissioning

Eurocore Industrial Company provides end-to-end HVAC solutions for the power sector, combining precision engineering with industrial-grade equipment to ensure optimal performance in high-heat environments. From initial technical estimation to final commissioning, we deliver systems that prioritize operational integrity and personnel safety.


03

Industrial kitchen & warehouse HVAC solutions

We provide comprehensive, end-to-end HVAC ducting solutions engineered for industrial kitchens and large-scale warehouse facilities. Combining high-performance steel and aluminum fabrication with expert technical estimation, we deliver ventilation systems that ensure thermal regulation, air quality, and operational safety. From initial design to final commissioning, our turnkey approach meets the highest industrial standards for efficiency and regulatory compliance.

04

Steel structural fabrication & erection

Eurocore Industrial Company provides comprehensive structural steel solutions, encompassing high-precision fabrication and expert onsite erection for large-scale industrial and commercial infrastructure. Our capabilities range from complex structural framing and heavy-duty supports to custom industrial assemblies, all engineered to meet the most stringent safety and load-bearing standards. By integrating advanced technical design with professional site management, we ensure that every structure—from labor camps to industrial plants—is delivered with superior structural integrity and operational excellence.

05

Solar worksite aluminium fabrication solutions

Eurocore Industrial Company delivers high-precision aluminum fabrication solutions specifically engineered for the structural demands of large-scale solar worksites. We specialize in the design, supply, and installation of durable, corrosion-resistant mounting structures and framing systems that maximize the longevity and performance of renewable energy infrastructure. By merging advanced technical fabrication with rigorous project management, we provide turnkey structural support tailored to the unique environmental conditions of utility-scale solar developments.

06

Aluminium & steel tank fabrication at facility

Eurocore Industrial Company delivers high-precision aluminum fabrication solutions specifically engineered for the structural demands of large-scale solar worksites. We specialize in the design, supply, and installation of durable, corrosion-resistant mounting structures and framing systems that maximize the longevity and performance of renewable energy infrastructure. By merging advanced technical fabrication with rigorous project management, we provide turnkey structural support tailored to the unique environmental conditions of utility-scale solar developments.

07

Power Plant Turnarounds & Shutdown Services

Eurocore Industrial Company provides comprehensive turnaround and shutdown services designed to minimize downtime and restore peak operational efficiency. We specialize in delivering rapid-response solutions and highly skilled technical manpower for critical maintenance, complex repairs, and strategic component replacements. By implementing rigorous project management and safety protocols during high-pressure windows, we ensure that power plants and industrial facilities transition from shutdown to full-scale production with maximum reliability and structural integrity.

08

Global Steel Trading & Industrial Procurement

Eurocore Industrial Company facilitates large-scale global steel trading, bridging the gap between world-class manufacturers and high-demand industrial sectors. We specialize in the bulk procurement of premium industrial steel grades, sourcing directly from audited production facilities in Malaysia, India, and China. Supported by robust international banking frameworks, including Letter of Credit (LC) and Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC) facilities, we ensure secure, transparent, and efficient cross-border transactions that provide our clients with cost-effective material solutions and guaranteed supply chain continuity.
