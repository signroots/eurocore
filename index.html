About Our Company

Precision Engineering Industrial Solutions Excellence Without Compromise

Eurocore Industrial Company is a premier engineering and fabrication firm deeply rooted in the Saudi Arabian industrial landscape. Since our inception in 2007, we have evolved from a local specialist into a comprehensive provider of fabrication, infrastructure, HVAC solutions, and global steel trading. Based in Dammam and Jubail, we serve the Kingdom's vital sectors with a commitment to technical rigor, safety, and international excellence.

Vision 2030 Commitment

Our Commitment to the Kingdom: Vision 2030 & Beyond

At Eurocore Industrial Company Ltd., our mission is deeply aligned with the transformative goals of Saudi Vision 2030. We are dedicated to building the Kingdom's future by providing world-class industrial infrastructure, precision engineering, and specialized HVAC solutions for the nation's most ambitious Giga-projects.
From the futuristic landscapes of NEOM to the world-class venues planned for Saudi FIFA 2034, we stand as a key strategic partner in the Kingdom's journey toward global leadership in innovation, sustainability, and industrial excellence.
By localized expertise and global standards, we ensure that every project we touch contributes to the resilient and diverse economy envisioned by the Kingdom's leadership.
